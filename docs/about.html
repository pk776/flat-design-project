About Flat Design

Flat design is a minimalist design approach that emphasizes usability and simplicity. It focuses on clean, bold visuals and a clear hierarchy of information.

Key Components of Flat Design

Color

Flat design utilizes a vibrant color palette with solid fills. Colors are chosen for their emotional impact and clarity, avoiding gradients and textures.

Shapes

Geometry plays a crucial role in flat design. It employs simple shapes like rectangles, circles, and polygons with slightly rounded corners to create a friendly and approachable aesthetic.

Imagery

Imagery in flat design is characterized by minimal outline icons and flat vector graphics. Photography is rarely used, and when it is, it features flat color overlays to maintain consistency.

Tone

The tone of flat design is clear, direct, and approachable. It communicates confidently but minimally, focusing on essential information without unnecessary embellishments.

Typography

Flat design employs sans-serif fonts to ensure readability and modernity. The typography hierarchy is clear, with distinct sizes and weights to guide the viewer's attention.
