Timeline of Flat Design

Explore the significant events that shaped the evolution of flat design.

2000 – The Rise of Flat Design

Designers began simplifying interfaces for clarity and usability.

2013 – Windows 8 Launch

Microsoft introduced a bold flat aesthetic that influenced UI trends.

2014 – Material Design

Google blended flat design with depth and motion principles.

2015 – Flat Design 2.0

Subtle shadows and gradients re-emerged while maintaining simplicity.

2020 – The Evolution Continues

Flat design merges with other styles to create modern, user-friendly interfaces.
