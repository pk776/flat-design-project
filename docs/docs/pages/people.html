Prominent Figures in Flat Design

Jonathan Ive

Jonathan Ive is a British designer known for his work at Apple, where he played a crucial role in the development of flat design principles in user interfaces.

Matias Duarte

Matias Duarte is a designer at Google, recognized for his contributions to Android's Material Design, which incorporates flat design elements.

Mike Kus

Mike Kus is a designer and illustrator known for his vibrant flat design work, emphasizing simplicity and clarity in digital interfaces.

Jessica Walsh

Jessica Walsh is a designer and partner at Sagmeister & Walsh, known for her bold and modern approach to flat design in branding and advertising.
