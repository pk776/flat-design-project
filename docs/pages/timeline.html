Timeline of Flat Design

Explore the significant events that shaped the evolution of flat design.

2000 - The Rise of Flat Design

Flat design began to gain popularity as designers sought to create simpler, more functional interfaces.

2013 - Windows 8 Launch

Microsoft's Windows 8 introduced a flat design aesthetic, influencing many other platforms and applications.

2014 - Material Design

Google introduced Material Design, which incorporated flat design principles while adding depth through shadows and layers.

2015 - Flat Design 2.0

Designers began to explore variations of flat design, incorporating subtle gradients and shadows while maintaining simplicity.

2020 - The Evolution Continues

Flat design continues to evolve, blending with other styles to create modern, user-friendly interfaces.
